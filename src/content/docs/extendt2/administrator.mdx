Administrators are users responsible for managing the participating members of
their educational institution. These members are educators and learners. An administrator
can add, delete or edit their institution members.

## Registering

The first time you visit the Exten(DT)² platform you will be greeted by the login page

</BrowserWindow>

By clicking <span class="px-1 font-bold text-lg text-[#362f78] uppercase rounded border border-neutral-200 drop-shadow-xl dark:bg-neutral-200">Login</span> the three user role specific buttons appear

<BrowserWindow url="extendt2.com">
	<Image
		src={ext_login_2}
		alt="The options for the three user roles"
		width={ext_login_2.width}
		height="auto"

By selecting <span class="kbd bg-[#543b8a] text-white gap-x-1"><img src="/docs/img/admin.png" class="!h-4" alt="" aria-hidden="true"/>ADMINISTRATOR</span>
you will be presented with the administrator options.

Click on <span class="kbd bg-[#362f78] text-white">Register</span> to register your institution administrator.

<BrowserWindow url="extendt2.com">
	<Image
		src={ext_login_3}

A dialog box appears with a form you must fill.

Enter the name of your institution and a valid e-mail address.

<Aside type="caution">
	It is important to enter a <b>valid e-mail address</b> you have access to as
	in this address you will receive a confirmation message with information on
	how to access your newly created account.
</Aside>

<BrowserWindow url="extendt2.com">
	<Image
		src={ext_login_4}
		alt="The administrator register form"
		width={ext_login_4.width}

	/>
	<span slot="caption">The administrator register form</span>

Click <span class="kbd bg-[#4a4284] text-white">OK</span>.

A message will appear suggesting you verify your email address.

![Message prompting you to verify your e-mail address](@images/extendt2/ext_login_5.png)
_Message prompting you to verify your e-mail address_

![Confirmation e-mail](@images/extendt2/ext_conf_email.png)
_Confirmation e-mail you will receive_

Follow the instructions on the e-mail message you received. After you have all the
necessary info you can proceed to logging in as administrator.

<Aside type="caution">
	Your confirmation email contains information on how to get your credentials.

    <strong>Pay attention to it!</strong>

</Aside>

## Logging in

As before, at the login page, select <span class="px-1 font-bold text-lg text-[#362f78] uppercase rounded border border-neutral-200 drop-shadow-xl dark:bg-neutral-200">Login</span>,
then <span class="kbd bg-[#543b8a] text-white"><img src="/docs/img/admin.png" class="!h-4 mr-2" alt="" aria-hidden="true"/>ADMINISTRATOR</span>
and then <span class="kbd bg-[#362f78] text-white">Login</span>.

A dialog box with the login form appears.

<BrowserWindow url="extendt2.com">
	<Image
		src={ext_login_6}
		alt="Administrator login form"
		width={ext_login_6.width}
		height="auto"
	/>
	<span slot="caption">Administrator login form</span>
</BrowserWindow>

Enter the e-mail address you used to register and your password and click <span class="kbd bg-[#4a4284] text-white">OK</span>.

If your credentials were correct you should see the **Administrator Panel** page.
Here, you can select to manage either your educators or your learners.

## Managing Educators

In the **Educators** page you can view all your registered educators.
At first the page will be empty as you have not registered any educators yet.

Click <span class="kbd bg-[#362f78] text-white">Add new educator</span> to add a new one.

<BrowserWindow url="extendt2.com">
	<Image
		src={ext_admin_edu_empty}
		alt="Educators empty page"
		width={ext_admin_edu_empty.width}
		height="auto"
	/>
	<span slot="caption">Educators empty page</span>
</BrowserWindow>

Fill all the fields with the appropriate information. Enter the educators e-mail in the
**Educator ID** field.

<BrowserWindow url="extendt2.com" noBottomMargin>
    <Image
        src={ext_admin_edu_create}
        alt="Add educator form page"
        width={ext_admin_edu_create.width}
        height="auto"
    />
    <span slot="caption">Add educator form page</span>
</BrowserWindow>

<Aside type="caution">
	In the <code>Educator ID</code> field you need to enter a valid e-mail
	address that the educator has access to.
	<strong>
		They will receive all the necessary information on that address!
	</strong>
</Aside>

Click <span class="kbd bg-[#362f78] text-white">Save</span> to add the new educator.

Now, your newly added educator should appear in the the **Registered Educators** page.
For each educator that is created an appropriate educator account is created.
See more at the [Educator page](/docs/extendt2/educator).

<BrowserWindow url="extendt2.com">
    <Image
        src={ext_admin_edu_first}
        alt="First educator entry"
        width={ext_admin_edu_first.width}
        height="auto"
    />
    <span slot="caption">First educator entry</span>
</BrowserWindow>

Notice that initially every educator is unverified.
**In order for the registration to be completed educators need to verify their account by following the instruction on the email they receive .**
If not, they cannot access their educator account.

<Aside type="caution">
	The confirmation email the educators receive contains information on how to
	get their credentials.

    <strong>Urge them to pay attention to it!</strong>

</Aside>

## Managing Learners

In the **Learners** page you can view all your registered learners.
At first the page will be empty as you have not registered any learners yet.

Click <span class="kbd bg-[#362f78] text-white">Add new learner</span> to add a new one.

<BrowserWindow url="extendt2.com">
    <Image
        src={ext_admin_lea_empty}
        alt="Registered Learners initially empty page"
        width={ext_admin_lea_empty.width}
        height="auto"
    />
    <span slot="caption">Registered Learners initially empty page</span>
</BrowserWindow>

Fill in the learner's name and click <span class="kbd bg-[#362f78] text-white">Save</span>
to add the new leaner.

<BrowserWindow url="extendt2.com">
    <Image
        src={ext_admin_lea_create}
        alt="Learner creation page"
        width={ext_admin_lea_create.width}
        height="auto"
    />
    <span slot="caption">Learner creation page</span>
</BrowserWindow>

Now, your newly added learner should appear in the the **Registered Learners** page.
For each learner that is created an appropriate learner account is created.

<BrowserWindow url="extendt2.com">
    <Image
        src={ext_admin_lea_first}
        alt="First learner entry"
        width={ext_admin_lea_first.width}
        height="auto"
    />
    <span slot="caption">First learner entry</span>
</BrowserWindow>

See more at the [Learner](/docs/extendt2/learner) page.

<Aside>
	Learner/Team ID is the ticket to the Exten(DT)² Platform. Make sure you or
	the educator shares the ID out with the respective learner(s).
</Aside>
